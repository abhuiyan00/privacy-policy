this project (the **"Service"**). It is written to align with the **EU/UK General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR/UK GDPR)** and the **California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by
the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA)**, and to be reusable across the projects
maintained by the author.

> **Plain-language summary.** This is a personal, non-commercial project. It does **not** run

> security, and (2) anything you choose to send by email. Personal data is **never sold or
> shared** for advertising. You have full rights over your data — see [Section 9](#9-your-privacy-rights).

## 3. Key terms

- **Personal data / personal information** — any information relating to an identified or
  identifiable person (for example a name, email address, or IP address).
- **Processing** — any operation performed on personal data (collecting, storing, using,
  disclosing, deleting).
- **Controller** — the party that decides why and how personal data is processed (see
  [Section 1](#1-who-we-are-data-controller)).
- **Processor** — a party that processes personal data on the controller's behalf (for
  example, the hosting provider).

## 9. Your privacy rights

Your rights depend on where you live. To exercise any of them, email
[farhanheisenberg360@gmail.com](mailto:farhanheisenberg360@gmail.com); we will respond within
the timeframe required by law (generally **30 days** under the GDPR and **45 days** under the
CCPA/CPRA). We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

### 9.1 EEA / UK residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)
You have the right to: **access** your data; request **rectification** of inaccurate data;
request **erasure** ("right to be forgotten"); **restrict** or **object** to processing;
**data portability**; and to **withdraw consent** at any time. You also have the right to
**lodge a complaint** with your local supervisory authority (in Poland, the *Urząd Ochrony
Danych Osobowych* — UODO).

### 9.2 California residents (CCPA / CPRA)
You have the right to: **know** what personal information is collected and how it is used;
**access** and obtain a copy; **delete** it; **correct** inaccurate information; **opt out** of
sale or sharing (not applicable here, as we do neither); **limit** the use of sensitive
personal information (we collect none); and to **non-discrimination** for exercising these
rights. You may use an authorised agent to submit a request.

### 9.3 Other jurisdictions
If your local law (for example Brazil's LGPD or Canada's PIPEDA) grants similar rights, we
will honour them to the extent they apply.
